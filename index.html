What's inside

Built only for animal records —
nothing you don't need.

No crop planning, no equipment schedules, no invoicing. Just a clean, fast way to keep track of every animal you raise.

🏷️

Animal profiles

Tag ID, name, breed, sex, date of birth, and status for every head of livestock or poultry you keep.

✚

Health & vaccination log

Timestamp checkups, vaccinations, deworming, treatments, and breeding events in a running timeline.

⚖️

Weight tracking

Log weight checks over time to keep an eye on growth and condition.

🗓️

Tasks & reminders

Schedule vaccinations and deworming rounds ahead of time, and see what's overdue at a glance.

📦

Feed & inventory

Track feed, medicine, and supplies with quick stock adjustments and low-stock alerts.

📊

Reports

See herd composition, status breakdowns, and health activity at a glance — no spreadsheets needed.

🔍

Search & filter

Find any animal instantly by tag number, name, breed, species, or status.

📋

Farm dashboard

Quick actions, upcoming tasks, herd mix, and recent activity — all on one screen.

🔒

Private by default

Your records are tied to your account only — nobody else sees your farm's data.

How it works

From new arrival to full history, in three steps.

1

Add the animal

Tag it as livestock or poultry, give it an ID, and fill in the basics — species, breed, sex, birth date.

2

Log what happens

Every checkup, vaccination, treatment, or weight check gets added to that animal's own timeline.

3

Track from the dashboard

Search, filter, and see farm-wide totals and recent activity whenever you check in.
